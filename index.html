About Us

We are a faith-based charity in Kampala, Uganda, dedicated to transforming lives through compassion and action.

Vision

To create a compassionate world where every orphan, elderly person, and vulnerable individual in Uganda is empowered through faith, community support, and sustainable development, fostering hope and dignity for all.

Mission

To spread the gospel of hope, provide essential support to orphans and the elderly, and aid vulnerable communities in Uganda. We align our efforts with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) to ensure lasting impact, focusing on poverty alleviation, health, education, and equality.

Goals

  • Support orphans with education and care, aligning with SDG 4 (Quality Education) and SDG 1 (No Poverty).
  • Provide aid to the elderly, including health services and companionship, supporting SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-Being) and SDG 10 (Reduced Inequalities).
  • Conduct gospel outreach programs to inspire spiritual growth, linked to SDG 4 for lifelong learning.
  • Assist vulnerable communities with clean water and basic needs, contributing to SDG 6 (Clean Water and Sanitation) and SDG 2 (Zero Hunger).
  • Build partnerships for sustainable community development, promoting SDG 17 (Partnerships for the Goals).
